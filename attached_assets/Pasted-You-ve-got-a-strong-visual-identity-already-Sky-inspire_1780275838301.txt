Biggest UX Win

If I were leading this redesign, I would make one change above all others:

Replace the card-heavy homepage with a single progression-driven experience.

Current feeling:

> "Here's everything."



Desired feeling:

> "Here's your next meaningful action."



The best social/wellness/gamified apps (Duolingo, Finch, Habitica, Sky) all obsess over showing the user exactly what to do next.

Your visual design is already close to production quality. The next leap is not better graphics—it's stronger hierarchy, fewer competing cards, and making the Constellation system the central narrative of the product.
