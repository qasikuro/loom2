### Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Sky Journal** is a calm, beautiful space to capture your sky memories, create manga-style stories, and softly connect with a community of sky wanderers.

Inspired by the gentle world of Sky: Children of the Light, the app offers three focused areas designed for quiet, meaningful self-expression — no noise, no pressure.

---

**JOURNAL — Your private sanctuary**
Write freely. Log your thoughts, moods, sky friend encounters, and the small moments that matter. A new writing prompt appears each day to guide your pen. All journal entries are completely private — only you can see them.

**STORIES — Manga panels, your way**
Create illustrated chapters up to 12 panels long. Upload sky photos, write narration over them, and publish your story to the discover feed. Or keep it private — a chapter written only for yourself.

**DISCOVER — A quiet social feed**
Browse stories from other sky wanderers. Filter by mood vibe: Soft, Lonely, Romantic, Chaotic, Peaceful, Adventurous, Dreamy, or Hopeful. Follow creators you love. "Witness" stories that move you (no likes — just witnessing). Search for sky friends by @username or display name.

The discovery algorithm quietly surfaces stories from people you follow, stories that match your current mood, and fresh voices from the wider sky community.

**CHARACTER & OUTFIT LOG**
Craft your sky identity: set your name, a short bio, and a unique @username handle. Add character trait chips. Log your daily outfits with photos, descriptions, and vibe tags. Toggle your profile public or private at any time.
